More About Me!

My name's Kirk, and I'm currently an instructor at Red Deer Polytechnic. I have a degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Alberta. I've lived in Central Alberta my whole life, save for going to university. Outside of school, I enjoy tinkering with electronics, collecting records, and playing curling. Some of my favourite bands are Pink Floyd, Rush, and the Eagles. I attended the Canada Wide Science Fair as a participant for 4 years in a row.
